# Software Engineering
Tags: engineering, development, practice, discipline

## Definition
A systematic, disciplined, and quantifiable approach to the design, development, operation, and maintenance of software.

## Key Aspects
- Focuses on the entire software lifecycle.
- Emphasizes principles of design, testing, quality assurance, and maintenance.
- Aims to produce reliable, efficient, and maintainable software systems.
- Involves managing complexity, resources, and team collaboration.
