# LLM Benchmarks
Tags: ai, evaluation, testing

## Definition
Standardized tests, datasets, and metrics used to evaluate the performance, capabilities, and limitations of Large Language Models (LLMs).

## Purpose
- Measure accuracy and performance on specific tasks (e.g., coding, reasoning).
- Compare different LLMs and model versions.
- Identify areas for improvement in LLM development.

## Examples
- HumanEval (coding)
- SWE-bench (coding)
- MMLU (multitask understanding)
