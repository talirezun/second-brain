Dr. Tali Rezun is the author of the article "Three Philosophies, One Goal; A Practitioner’s Comparison of Augment Code, Claude Code, and Codex CLI." He is an experienced practitioner in AI-assisted development, comparing various AI coding tools based on extensive hands-on experience.
